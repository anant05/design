✓ Full edit by author
✓ Delete initiative
→ Submit for CI review
✗ Not visible to CI
SubmittedAwaiting CI triage
Author has submitted the initiative to the CI team for feasibility review. Author can still edit or retract while awaiting triage.
✓ Full edit by author
✓ Retract to Draft
✓ CI team can see it
→ CI moves to Review
ReviewCI evaluating feasibility
CI team reviews the initiative, schedules a meeting with stakeholders, and assesses the technical feasibility and work involved.

🔒 Scope locked
→ CI → Ready
