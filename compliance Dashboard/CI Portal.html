Draft
Ownership, feasibility and communication plan are confirmed with stakeholders. Still internal — no project teams are notified.
3
Backend + CI
Draft
The CI backend team implements and verifies enforcement hooks. Pipeline checks are configured and validated before any rollout decision is made.

Test
Run in warning-only or mixed mode. Teams can see compliance status but PRs are not blocked. Validates behavior and reduces rollout risk before going live.
Active State — Production Enforcement
5
Active — Enforcement Off
Off
Initiative is active in production but CI enforcement has not started. Teams can see the initiative but checks don't affect PRs yet.
5
Active — Warn Mode
Warn
CI checks run on every PR. Non-compliant PRs surface a warning but are not blocked. Teams see their compliance status and have time to remediate.
5
Active — Blocking Mode
Block
Non-compliant PRs are blocked from merging. Exceptions must be approved through the compliance portal. Full enforcement is active.
